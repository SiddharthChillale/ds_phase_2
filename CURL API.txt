CURL APIS
1. Init Publisher: curl --location --request GET 'http://localhost:80/initPublisher'
2. Generate Client: curl --location --request POST 'http://localhost:80/generateClient'
3. Subscribe: curl --location --request POST 'http://localhost:80/subscribe/7/1'
4. Unsubscribe: curl --location --request POST 'http://localhost:80/unsubscribe/7/1'
